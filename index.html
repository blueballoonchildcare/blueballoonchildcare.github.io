Welcome to Blue Baloon Childcare

Hi, My name is Dawn Hester. I am married to Kevin and mother to twins Taya and Cian born August 2009. Blue Baloon Childcare's ethos is to provide a homely setting where children can play and learn and have fun! I live in a 3 bedroom house in a quiet road on the Frimley/Camberley border. It has a large fully enclosed garden with outdoor toys and a vegetable patch. Indoors the children have access to 2 playrooms.

I decided to become a childminder as I wanted to stay at home with my children but still do what I love, creating a fun and caring place for children to learn and watch them blossom. I am OFSTED registered with a satisfactory/good rating and a member of PACEY.
